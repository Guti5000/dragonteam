 ===========================================================================================
					-DESCRIPTION-
 ===========================================================================================

		     A program to edit the Pokédex Data in GBA Pokémon ROMs.
                      

 ===========================================================================================
				       -ROMs SUPPORTED-
 ===========================================================================================
		
	              At the moment only English R/S/FR/LG/E ROMs are supported.		

 ===========================================================================================
				       -INSTRUCTIONS-
 ===========================================================================================

 1) Open the ROM you want to edit.
 2) Click on the Pokémon whose Pokédex Data you want to edit.
 3) Then change what you would like to change.
 4) Click "Save" and your done!
 
*Note*	If you click on another Pokémon in the listbox the data for the previous one will 
not be saved. Make sure to Click "Save" before you click on another Pokémon.
