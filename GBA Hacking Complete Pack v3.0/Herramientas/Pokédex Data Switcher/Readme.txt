 ===========================================================================================
					-DESCRIPTION-
 ===========================================================================================

		     A program to Switch and edit the Pokédex Data in GBA Pokémon ROMs.
                      

 ===========================================================================================
				       -ROMs SUPPORTED-
 ===========================================================================================
		
	              At the moment only English R/S/FR/LG/E ROMs are supported.		

 ===========================================================================================
				       -INSTRUCTIONS-
 ===========================================================================================

 1) Open the ROM you want to edit.
 2) Click on the first Pokémon whose Pokédex Data you want to switch/edit.
 3) Then do the same for the second.
 4) Change what you want and save a side if desired.
 5) Click "<- Switch ->" and your done! (Optional)
